Information

  • Equatorial Radius - Equatorial radius of the planet. Leaving it as 0 will calculate no parallax.
  • Polar Radius - Polar radius of the planet. Leaving it as 0 will calculate no parallax.
  • Semi Major Axis - Semi major axis of the planet's orbit.
  • Year Length - Orbital period of the planet, in planet days.
  • Eccentricity - Eccentricity of the planet's orbit. Must be between 0 (inclusive) and 1 (exclusive).
  • Axial Tilt - Axial tilt of the planet, in degrees.
  • Arg. of Perigee - Longitude of the Sun when the planet is at perihelion, in degrees. This is equal to the argument of perihelion + 180 degrees.
  • Perihelion Date - Date of the year when the planet is at perihelion, in planet days.
  • Hours in Day - Number of subdivisions within one planet day. Must be an integer.
  • Twilight Increment - Degrees of solar altitude in between each twilight.
  • Latitude - Latitude of the observing site, in degrees.
  • Longitude - Longitude of the observing site, in degrees.
  • Date - Date of observation, in planet days.
  • Timezone - Timezone of the observing site, in planet hours.
  • Auto Timezone - Automatically calculate the timezone of the observing site. Overrides "Timezone".
  • No Timezones - No timezones are used. Uses pure and exact local time. Overrides "Timezone" and "Auto Timezone".
